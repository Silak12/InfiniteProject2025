What is The Infinite Canvas?

TikTok Art Project

The original artwork is created on TikTok @The-infiniteCanvas. The account feed assembles the artwork image by image, creating an endless collaborative masterpiece where each piece builds upon the last.

Free to Participate

Participation is completely free! Apply through our contact form and become part of this unique artistic journey.

Requirements

No AI Generated Art

Every image must be created by human hands. Whether digital, traditional painting, or any other medium - we celebrate human creativity.

Exact Format Required

Every image must be drawn in the exact format: 9:11.91 (width x height)

Important: The sizes below are just examples - you can choose any size as long as the 9:11.91 ratio is maintained.

Artist Signature Required

Every artist must sign their artwork. Your signature becomes part of the infinite canvas and helps preserve the collaborative history.

Tips & Tricks

For Analog Artists

Print the adjacent images in the size you want to draw your own image. This helps achieve better transitions and continuity in the collaborative artwork.

Perfect Transitions Not Required

Transitions don't need to be 100% perfect! The better they work, the more beautiful the result, but imperfections add character to the collaborative piece.

Keep Your Unique Style

Every artist should paint in their own style! The diversity of styles is what makes The Infinite Canvas special and dynamic.

How It Works

7-Day Time Frame

When it's your turn, you have 7 days to complete and submit your artwork. If this timeframe is exceeded, the spot moves to the next artist to keep the project flowing.

One Image Per Round

Each artist creates one image initially, but can participate again later in the project. Early contributors who help launch the project may get priority for future rounds.

Artist Credits

Artists receive full credit for their work. On this website, hovering over your image shows your link. On TikTok, you'll be tagged and mentioned in captions, ensuring your contribution is always recognized.
